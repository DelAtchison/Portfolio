Hi, I'm Adele Atchison

I'm an Illustrator, Designer, Front End Web Developer and Artist.

I'm a multidisciplinary designer and I graduated from the University of Ulster with a 1st Class BA Hons in Interactive Media Arts.   I have many skills to bring to any project I work on, ranging from front end coding or web design, to creating content such as illustrations, photos or video content.
I hope you enjoy viewing my portfolio which I built myself, and wordpress blog that I created the theme for from the ground up.

My mission as a creative is to make work that conveys messages and inspires emotions in the viewer.   I use both digital and traditional mediums in my work, ensuring I can use the strengths of both to maximise the effects I seek to create.

For more information on any of my work or experience get in touch via my contact form below, I'm happy to answer questions and I love to help!   CV available on request.

I also do motion graphics/animation, check out my animation reel!
